Section 1

What INFORMATIV Is

A psycholinguistic advertising intelligence system whose power comes from the conversion edge — the match between a psychologically annotated ad and a psychologically profiled buyer at the moment of verified purchase.
The Core Insight
Customer reviews are the most honest expression of buyer psychology ever recorded. 937 million people told us WHO they are (through the language they used), WHAT persuaded them (through what they praised), and WHETHER it worked (through the purchase outcome). We annotated both sides — buyer psychology AND seller positioning — at 65 dimensions each, then computed the 27-dimensional alignment between them. This is the world's only dataset with both sides annotated at psychological depth.

The Bilateral Edge

Every advertising system in existence measures the surface: clicks, impressions, demographics. INFORMATIV measures the DEPTH — the psychological structure of why a specific person converts for a specific product.

Cognitive Retargeting Engine

Not "show the same ad again." Clinical psychology-grade retargeting that diagnoses WHY someone didn't convert, then deploys the specific intervention to resolve that barrier.
What Makes This Different
Standard retargeting: "They visited your site. Show them the same ad 7 more times."

INFORMATIV retargeting: "They visited your site but didn't convert because their brand_trust_fit is 0.32 (below the 0.37 conversion threshold for their archetype). Their negativity_bias_match is 0.47 (too high — they read negative reviews and froze). Deploy anxiety_resolution on a high-authority editorial page where the publisher's credibility transfers to the ad. This is Touch 3 of 5, narrative chapter 3 (Rising Action), scaffold level 3 (Direction Maintenance). If this touch fails, the next touch will use a DIFFERENT mechanism targeting a DIFFERENT barrier."

Section 4

Resonance Engineering

The page where your ad appears isn't neutral. It creates a psychological field that amplifies or dampens your message. Resonance Engineering computes the optimal page environment for every impression.
The Third Edge
INFORMATIV's bilateral edge measures buyer × seller alignment. But every impression happens IN a psychological environment created by the page. An article about airline delays primes FRUSTRATION — frustration_framing mechanisms are amplified, action-satiation-seeking is elevated. A luxury resort review primes ACTION & CONTROL — status mechanisms resonate. Resonance Engineering adds the third edge: buyer × seller × page. When all three frequencies align, the signal is amplified beyond what any two alone produce. When they're out of phase, the signal cancels.

Six-Layer Architecture

SENSE
Extract 32-Dimension Page Mindstate
Every page is scored on 32 psychological dimensions: 20 bilateral alignment dimensions (same space as the conversion edges), 7 NDF construct activations, and 5 environmental scalars. Per-ARTICLE, not per-domain — an article about delays creates a different mindstate than one about first-class lounges on the same site.
MODEL
Compute Resonance Multiplier: R(buyer, seller, page)
For each (barrier × mechanism × archetype), computes how the page's mindstate amplifies or dampens conversion probability. Three stages: Theory Prior (day 1) → Empirical Logistic Regression (after 50 outcomes) → Neural Network (after 500 outcomes). The model auto-progresses per cell.
MATCH
Dynamic Placement Optimization
Given this buyer, this barrier, this mechanism: compute the IDEAL page mindstate → translate to StackAdapt bid multipliers. Per-archetype, per-touch domain targeting. Replaces static whitelists with dynamically evolving placement that improves with every impression.
ADAPT
Real-Time Creative Adjustment
Same mechanism, different execution: evidence_proof on an analytical page → data and statistics. Evidence_proof on an emotional page → testimonial and personal story. The creative rotates to align with the page's psychological field. Pre-computed for <5ms bid-time execution.
LEARN
Every Outcome Updates Resonance Posteriors
Conversion on an analytical page with evidence_proof? The system learns: "analytical pages amplify evidence mechanisms." This updates the resonance model, the hierarchical priors, the Neural-LinUCB context, and the gradient fields — simultaneously at all 5 hierarchy levels.
EVOLVE
Active Self-Improvement
The system generates HYPOTHESES about novel page × mechanism combinations ("Does anxiety_resolution work on POSITIVE pages via contrast?"), allocates 10% of impressions to testing them, detects non-obvious synergies, promotes winners, prunes losers, and introduces mutations. Not pattern recognition — active evolutionary learning.

Every campaign is a learning experiment. Here's what we expect and how we'll measure it.
Core Hypothesis
Each subsequent retargeting touch should convert at a HIGHER rate than the previous — because each touch targets a more specific psychological barrier with a more precisely matched mechanism.
If Touch N converts lower than Touch N-1, the mechanism mapping is wrong for that archetype and the system recalibrates automatically.
