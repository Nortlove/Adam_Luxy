Why It's Different

🧠
Causal, not correlational
Other systems see "authority ads work on news sites." We know authority works because the page creates information-seeking + low reactance. That means authority works on ANY page with those conditions — even a health page or product review. We generalize through understanding.
🔗
Both sides of the equation
We have the buyer's psychology (from their review) AND the seller's psychology (from the product listing) for 47 million actual purchases. No one else has both sides. It's like having the answer key for every ad decision.
📈
Self-teaching system
Every ad impression is a micro-experiment. The system runs statistical tests on its own outcomes to discover new causal relationships and writes them back into the graph. It doesn't just learn — it learns how to learn.

No cookies needed
Privacy regulations are killing cookie-based targeting. Our system reads the page psychology, not the user's browsing history. It works without any personal data — and it's more accurate than cookies ever were.
