THE PROBLEM

Every major ad platform predicts behavior and choice from weak correlations.

Correlations are misleading, lack insight, and don't transfer. The entire industry optimizes on signals that can't explain, generalize, or adapt.

CORRELATIONAL APPROACH
  • "Female, 25-34, visited beauty sites"
  • "Browsed running shoes last week"
  • "Located in New York metro area"
  • Show the same ad to everyone who matches
  • Can't explain why anything works
MECHANISM-DRIVEN APPROACH
  • "Prevention-focused, seeks reliability over novelty"
  • "Trusts expert endorsement, skeptical of hype"
  • "Reading content that activates competence goals"
  • Match the message to the buyer's psychology
  • Every recommendation has a causal explanation
