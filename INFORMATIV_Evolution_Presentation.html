Advertising That Understands Psychology,

Every major ad platform predicts what you'll buy based on what you've already done. INFORMATIV predicts what you'll buy based on who you are.

Conventional Pattern Matching

Traditional platforms correlate past actions to predict future ones. "Users who clicked X also clicked Y." This breaks down for new products, new markets, and any situation where the past doesn't predict the future.

INFORMATIV Psychological Inference

We analyze 937 million product reviews to extract both sides of every purchase decision — what the buyer was feeling and what the seller was doing. This creates the world's only bilateral psychological evidence base for advertising.

Why This Matters for Investors

The bilateral evidence base is a compounding asset. Every impression teaches the system something new. Every conversion refines 22 interconnected learning systems. Unlike demographic targeting, which has diminishing returns as it saturates, psychological targeting gets more precise with every interaction — and the learnings transfer across brands and categories.

8-Week Evolution

From Research Platform to
Production Intelligence Engine

A chronological view of the capabilities added, the methodologies adopted, and the competitive moat deepened — week by week.

Late February 2026
Foundation: The Bilateral Evidence Architecture

Built the core data structure that makes everything else possible: bilateral alignment edges. For each of 6.75 million purchase events, we extracted 27 psychological dimensions from both the buyer's review and the seller's product description — creating a two-sided understanding of why each purchase happened.

This is analogous to studying both sides of a clinical trial — not just whether the patient recovered, but the precise mechanism of action that caused recovery.

Early March 2026
The 5-Level Decision Cascade

Developed a progressive-depth decision engine that adapts its sophistication to available evidence:

Level 1 (2ms): Population-level psychological priors
Level 2 (10ms): Category-specific refinement
Level 3 (30ms): Direct bilateral edge evidence — the core differentiator
Level 4 (100ms): Cross-category transfer learning
Level 5 (500ms): Full AI reasoning with 30 psychological modules

Most decisions reach Level 3 for LUXY Ride, meaning creative recommendations come from actual purchase evidence, not theoretical models.

March 28-29, 2026
Production Hardening + Causal Intelligence

Transformed the research platform into production infrastructure: 120ms response guarantee, automatic failover, persistent learning across restarts. But the real breakthrough was causal intelligence — the system now identifies the 3-5 specific psychological factors that caused each conversion, not just which factors were present.

This borrows from clinical research methodology: instead of asking "did the treatment work?", we ask "which specific mechanism of action drove the result, and how can we reproduce it?"

March 30, 2026
Per-Person Learning + Adaptive Retargeting

Adopted within-subject experimental design — a methodology from clinical trials where each person serves as their own control group. Instead of comparing different users, we track how each individual responds across multiple touchpoints.

Result: the system learns 2-4x faster about returning users and can prescribe not just what creative to show, but on which type of page to show it — matching the psychological state the page creates with the mechanism the ad deploys.

April 3, 2026
Signal Intelligence: Separating Signal from Noise

Built six behavioral sensors that measure how someone processes an ad, not just whether they clicked:

  • Processing Depth — Did they actually see the ad, or was it below the fold for less than a second? This single sensor eliminates 30-50% of measurement noise.
  • Click Hesitation Analysis — Fast clicks indicate automatic interest. Slow clicks with pauses indicate deliberation or approach-avoidance conflict.
  • Organic Return Detection — When someone comes back to a brand on their own (without seeing an ad), it signals internal motivation formation.
  • Ad Fatigue Detection — Individual-level resistance tracking that catches message fatigue before it leads to brand damage.

The processing depth sensor alone represents a step-change: learning systems that previously trained on 100% of impressions now train on only the 50-70% that were actually processed. Cleaner data produces exponentially better learning.

April 3, 2026 (continued)
Neural Contextual Decision-Making

Promoted our neural contextual bandit to the primary mechanism selector. This system uses 57 dimensions of context — including all 27 bilateral alignment scores, 6 behavioral signals, 7 compressed psychological factors, and real-time device/temporal context — to select the optimal persuasion approach for each individual impression.

Selection latency: 0.29 milliseconds. This is 100x faster than the latency budget, meaning the system makes psychologically-informed decisions at the speed of programmatic bidding.

April 12-13, 2026
Nonconscious Goal Activation

This was the theoretical breakthrough that reframed the entire platform. Based on 30 years of peer-reviewed research on automatic goal pursuit, we built a system that understands how page content activates nonconscious goals in the reader — before any ad is shown.

Key insight: when someone reads an article about executive productivity, it doesn't just put them in a "business" topic category. It automatically activates goals around competence, status, and time efficiency. An ad for premium transportation fulfills those already-active goals — the person notices it automatically because their goal-directed attention system is primed for solutions.

The system profiles every page for which of 8 foundational goals it activates, then matches ads to goal fulfillment — moving from "topic relevance" to "goal completion."

April 15-16, 2026
Complete System Integration + Quality Assurance

The most intensive build session: 19 commits integrating every subsystem into a unified, fully-wired platform. Key completions:

  • 30 psychological reasoning modules operating in parallel, each contributing construct-level evidence to the final decision
  • 20 scoring dimensions sourced entirely from real conversion data — zero theoretical defaults remaining
  • Counterfactual learning: every impression now teaches the system about all possible approaches, not just the one deployed — multiplying learning speed by approximately 4x
  • Correlation-aware barrier diagnosis: when identifying why someone hasn't converted, the system now considers how fixing one barrier propagates improvements to others through the psychological correlation structure
  • Loss aversion integration: scoring now reflects the well-established finding that psychological losses hurt 2.25x more than equivalent gains help — meaning small deficits in trust or credibility are appropriately weighted
